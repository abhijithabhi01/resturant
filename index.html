About Us

Reinventing Shawarma is a passion that you can taste and one we’re proud to share.

very menu item is made fresh and made to be the best you’ve ever tasted. Zam Zam’s is a fast casual shawarma chain born in Turavur, edapally, on a mission to modernize the shawarma in unspeakable ways while also helping to spread happiness and the appetite for good food and good flavour in communities.

Great Food Comes First

Every day, more than 10,000 guests visit zam zam restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 2018, zam zam is the second largest fast food shawarma chain in the world. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 5 successful years.
